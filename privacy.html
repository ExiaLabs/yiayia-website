Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2 April 2026 · Effective: 30 March 2026

Yiayia — Greek Coffee Readings ("the App") is developed and operated by Nick Fielden ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Yiayia — Greek Coffee Readings, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID-provided email address (which may be a private relay address) and your display name (if you choose to share it). We do not collect your real name, phone number, or physical address.

1.2 Photos You Provide

When you take or upload a photo of your coffee cup for a reading, the image is uploaded to our secure servers for AI analysis, stored in your account so you can revisit past readings, and never shared with other users or third parties for marketing purposes. You may delete your photos and readings at any time from within the App.

1.3 Reading Data

We store the coffee cup readings generated for you, including the reading text and associated metadata (category, mood, symbols), timestamps of when readings were created, and your reading history and usage counts.

1.4 Usage Data

We collect basic usage information to maintain and improve the App: number of readings used per billing period (for free tier limits), subscription status, and app crash and error reports.

1.5 Purchase Information

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or financial information. We receive only confirmation of your subscription status from Apple.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide coffee cup readings based on your coffee cup photos, maintain your reading history, enforce free tier usage limits (3 readings per month), manage your subscription status, improve app performance and fix bugs, and respond to support requests.

We do not use your information for advertising or ad targeting, selling to third parties, profiling or behavioural tracking beyond the App, or training AI models on your personal photos or readings.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

We use Supabase for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers. Supabase Privacy Policy

3.2 AI Services (Reading Generation)

Your coffee cup photos are sent to third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude and/or Google Gemini) to generate coffee cup readings. These services process your image to produce the reading text. The AI providers' terms prohibit them from using your data to train their models.

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

3.3 Apple (Sign in with Apple & Subscriptions)

Authentication and payment processing are handled by Apple. Apple Privacy Policy

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or social media integrations.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers provided by Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). We implement Row-Level Security (RLS) ensuring users can only access their own data, encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication via Sign in with Apple, and API keys stored server-side and never exposed to the client app.

5. Data Retention

Your account data and reading history are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (profile, readings, photos) will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You may delete individual readings at any time from within the App.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access your data (view all readings and account information within the App), delete your data (delete individual readings or your entire account via the Account screen), download your data (request a copy through the "Download My Data" option in Account Settings), and withdraw consent (stop using the App at any time; delete your account to remove all data).

7. Children's Privacy

Yiayia — Greek Coffee Readings is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

Your data may be processed and stored in regions where our infrastructure providers operate (including the United States and the European Union). By using the App, you consent to this transfer of data.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your data is consent (you consent to photo processing when you submit a coffee cup image), contract (processing necessary to provide the reading service you've requested), and legitimate interest (basic usage analytics to maintain and improve the App).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
