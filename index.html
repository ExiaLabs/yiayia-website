Yiayia Coffee Fortune

Greek Coffee Cup Readings

Welcome to Yiayia's kitchen — where the ancient Greek tradition of coffee cup reading comes alive on your iPhone.

For centuries, Greek grandmothers have peered into the swirling patterns of coffee grounds to read fortunes for their families. Yiayia Coffee Fortune brings this cherished tradition to you, wherever you are in the world.

Photograph your Greek coffee cup, choose a category — Love, Fortune, or Health — and let Yiayia reveal what the grounds have to say.

"Ah, sit with your old Yiayia. Bring me your cup, and I'll whisper to you what the ancient ones reveal."
