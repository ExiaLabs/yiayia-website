Frequently Asked Questions

How does Yiayia read my coffee cup?

Take a photo of the inside of your Greek or Turkish coffee cup after the grounds have settled. Our AI analyses the patterns in the grounds and generates a personalised coffee cup reading in the voice of Yiayia, a fictional Greek grandmother character.

How many free readings do I get?

Free accounts receive 3 readings per calendar month. Your reading count resets on the 1st of each month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited readings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Yiayia — Greek Coffee Readings and tap Cancel Subscription. Your premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app, go to the Account tab, and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes your profile, all readings, and all uploaded photos within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

Are the coffee cup readings real?

Yiayia — Greek Coffee Readings is for entertainment purposes only. The readings are AI-generated content inspired by the Greek cultural tradition of tasseography (coffee cup reading). They should not be relied upon for making life decisions.

What happens to my photos?

Your coffee cup photos are stored securely on our servers and linked to your account. They are used only to generate your reading and are never shared with other users or used for advertising. You can delete individual readings (and their photos) at any time, or delete your entire account.
